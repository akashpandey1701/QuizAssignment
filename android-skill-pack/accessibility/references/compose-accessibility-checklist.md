# Compose Accessibility Checklist

## Semantics

- Are buttons, toggles, progress indicators, and images announced appropriately?
- Are merged or cleared semantics used intentionally rather than accidentally?

## Layout

- Do screens remain readable at larger font scales?
- Do controls retain usable touch targets after custom styling?
- Does landscape or multi-pane layout preserve focus clarity?

## Interaction

- Can users complete key flows with TalkBack and keyboard navigation where relevant?
- Are validation errors and async state changes announced clearly enough?

## Visuals

- Is contrast adequate for text, icons, and states?
- Is important meaning communicated through text, shape, or position in addition to color?
